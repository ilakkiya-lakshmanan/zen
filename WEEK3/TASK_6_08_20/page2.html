Vivo will not be IPL title sponsors this year, says BCCI

“The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) and Vivo Mobile India Pvt Ltd have decided to suspend their partnership for Indian Premier League in 2020,” the press release stated. Vivo won the IPL title sponsorship rights for five years from 2018 to 2022 for a reported sum of ₹2190 crore, approximately ₹440 crore per annum

The BCCI sent out a one-line statement without giving any details to say that Vivo will not be associated with the IPL this year.
